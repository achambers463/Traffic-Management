About Traffic Management

Traffic Management (AFSC 2T0X1) is responsible for coordinating and executing the movement of personnel, cargo, and equipment across the Air Force Enterprise. At the 23d Logistics Readiness Squadron (23d LRS), Traffic Management ensures strategic and tactical mobility support for the 23d Wing mission at Moody Air Force Base, GA and deployed locations worldwide.

23d Wing Mission

The 23d Wing provides rapid global mobility and precision engagement, ensuring readiness to execute joint and combined operations anytime, anywhere. Traffic Management plays a key role in enabling mission success by facilitating cargo and personnel movements in support of global taskings.
